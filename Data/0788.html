Dear Burkhardt

For air pressure at ozone maximum (Pa) I would put

   float pmaxo3(time, lat, lon) ;
      pmaxo3:standard_name = "air_pressure";
      pmaxo3:cell_methods = "mole_fraction_of_ozone_in_air: maximum" ;
      pmaxo3:units = "Pa" ;

The idea is to think of this as 4D field
air_pressure(time, mole_fraction_of_ozone_in_air, lat, lon)
in which the ozone dimension has been collapsed.

Best wishes

Jonathan
