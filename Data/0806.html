Dear Frederique

The Levitus (1994) atlas, for instance, calculates mixed layer depths every-
where by both temperature and density criteria. In GCMs with a mixed layer
scheme such as Kraus-Turner, the ML depth reports how deep the mixing
penetrated. These are all different things and I believe they might all be
reported and compared for a given region, so I think we need to distinguish
them.

Best wishes

Jonathan
